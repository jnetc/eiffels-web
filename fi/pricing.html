Usein Kysytyt Kysymykset

  • How can I create my first offer in marketplace?

    To create your first offer in the marketplace, follow these steps:

    • Log in to Your Account: Access your marketplace account.
    • Navigate to the Marketplace Section: Find and click on the Add New button.
    • Enter Offer Details: Provide all necessary information, including the title, description, pricing, and any relevant images.
    • Set Availability: Specify the terms, conditions, and availability of your offer.
    • Review and Publish: Double-check your information and click Publish to make your offer live.

    If you need any assistance, our support team is here to help! support@eiffels.com

  • Which payments we accept?

    We accept a wide range of payment methods to accommodate different needs:

    • For Individuals: Payments can be made using major credit and debit cards, digital wallets like PayPal or Apple Pay, and bank transfers.
    • For Companies: We offer options for corporate billing, including invoicing with payment terms, wire transfers, and credit card payments.
    • For Enterprise Clients: Custom payment solutions are available, including bulk invoicing, ACH transfers, and dedicated account management for large-scale transactions.

    If you have specific payment requirements or need further assistance, please contact our support team at support@eiffels.com for more details.

  • How do I upgrade to Standard plan?

    To upgrade to the Standard Plan, follow these steps:

    • Log in to your account.
    • Navigate to the "Plans and Pricing" section in your account settings.
    • Select the Standard plan from the available options.
    • Review the features and pricing, then click Upgrade.
    • Enter your payment information and confirm your upgrade.

    Once completed, your account will immediately switch to the Standard plan, unlocking all included features.
